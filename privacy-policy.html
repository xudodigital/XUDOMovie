1. Introduction

Welcome to XUDOMovie ("we", "us", "our"), operated by XUDODigital. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit xudomovie.us and any related pages or subdomains (collectively, the "Site").

By using our Site, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Site.

Summary: XUDOMovie does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email, or personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily submit it through our Contact form.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of data, primarily through automated means:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, browser type, operating system, referring URL, and approximate geographic location (country/region level), collected automatically via Google Analytics.
  • Device Information: Screen resolution, device type (mobile/desktop), and browser language settings — used solely for analytics and UI optimisation.
  • Voluntarily Submitted Data: If you use our Contact form, we collect the information you provide (name, email address, and message content). This data is transmitted to our email system and is not stored on the Site itself.

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, government-issued IDs, or any other sensitive personal data.

3. Local Storage & Browser Data

XUDOMovie uses your browser's localStorage to save your personal preferences and usage state locally on your own device. This data never leaves your browser and is never transmitted to our servers.

Data stored in localStorage includes:

  • xudo_favs: Your saved Favorites list (title, poster, and content ID).
  • xudo_watch_later: Your Watch Later list.
  • xudo_history: Your watch progress data (percentage watched per title).
  • xudo_adblock_dismissed: Whether you dismissed the AdBlock notice, so it is not shown again within 24 hours.
  • xudo_theme: Your preferred UI theme (dark or light mode).

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or localStorage via your browser settings.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

XUDOMovie itself does not set first-party cookies for tracking purposes. However, third-party services embedded on the Site — including Google Analytics, advertising partners, and TMDB — may set cookies on your device.

Types of cookies that may be present:

  • Analytics Cookies: Set by Google Analytics to track aggregated user behaviour across sessions.
  • Advertising Cookies: Set by advertising partners to serve and measure ads. These may include interest-based targeting cookies.
  • Functionality Cookies: Set by embedded video players (e.g., YouTube, for trailers) to remember playback preferences.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of embedded content such as trailers.

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (Google LLC, USA) to understand how visitors interact with the Site. Google Analytics collects anonymised data including pages viewed, session duration, traffic source, and device information. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you.

Google may use this data in accordance with their own privacy policy, available at policies.google.com/privacy.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

6. Third-Party Services

XUDOMovie integrates with the following third-party services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB): Provides all movie and TV show metadata, images, and ratings. TMDB Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Loads the Saira typeface from Google's servers. Google Privacy Policy
  • YouTube (Google LLC): Used to embed official movie and TV trailers. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics: Used for site analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Third-party Streaming Embeds: The streaming player embeds content from third-party platforms. XUDOMovie does not control these services and their privacy practices may differ.

7. Advertising

XUDOMovie displays advertising banners to help fund the free service. Ads are served through third-party advertising partners who may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your interests.

We do not sell your personal data to advertisers. Advertisers receive aggregated demographic or interest data, not your individual identity.

You may opt out of interest-based advertising by visiting aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.eu (EU users).

8. Children's Privacy

XUDOMovie is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted personal data to us through the Contact form, please contact us immediately and we will delete that information.

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites, including advertising links. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any third-party site you visit.

10. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of any personal data you have submitted (e.g., via the Contact form).
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Add-on to stop being tracked.
  • Clear Local Data: Clear your browser's localStorage at any time to remove all locally saved preferences and watch history.
  • Cookie Control: Use your browser settings or a consent management tool to control which cookies are set.

For EU/EEA residents, you may exercise rights under GDPR by contacting us. For California residents, you may exercise rights under CCPA similarly.

To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at contact@xudomovie.us.
