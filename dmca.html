1. Overview & Applicability

XUDOMovie ("we", "us", "our") respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects its users to do the same. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act of 1998 ("DMCA"), 17 U.S.C. § 512, we will respond promptly to claims of copyright infringement committed using our platform.

This policy applies to content hosted directly on xudomovie.us, including but not limited to text, images, descriptions, and other metadata. It does not apply to content streamed through third-party embeds, which are not under our control.

2. How to Submit a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that content hosted on XUDOMovie infringes your copyright, you may submit a written DMCA takedown notice to our designated Copyright Agent via email.

To be valid and actioned promptly, your notice must be in writing, sent to dmca@xudomovie.us, and contain all of the required information listed in Section 3 below.

⚠️ Warning: Under 17 U.S.C. § 512(f), any person who knowingly materially misrepresents that material is infringing may be subject to liability for damages, including costs and attorney's fees. Only submit a notice if you have a good-faith belief that the use is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.

3. Required Information

Your DMCA takedown notice must include all of the following elements to be valid under 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3):

Mandatory Elements of a DMCA Notice

  • ✓ Identification of the copyrighted work: A description or list of the copyrighted work(s) you claim has been infringed. If multiple works are covered by a single notification, a representative list is sufficient.
  • ✓ Identification of the infringing material: The specific URL(s) on xudomovie.us where the allegedly infringing material is located, with sufficient detail for us to locate it.
  • ✓ Your contact information: Your full name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.
  • ✓ Good-faith statement: A statement that you have a good-faith belief that the use of the material is not authorised by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  • ✓ Accuracy statement: A statement that the information in the notice is accurate, and under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorised to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
  • ✓ Signature: An electronic or physical signature of the copyright owner or a person authorised to act on their behalf.

Notices that are incomplete or do not comply with the requirements of 17 U.S.C. § 512(c)(3) will not be processed. We reserve the right to request additional information to verify the validity of any notice.

4. Our Response Process

Upon receipt of a valid and complete DMCA takedown notice, we will:

  1. Acknowledge receipt of your notice via email within 3–5 business days.
  2. Review the notice for completeness and validity. Incomplete notices may be returned for correction.
  3. Investigate the claim by reviewing the identified URL(s) and the nature of the alleged infringement.
  4. Take appropriate action, which may include removal or disabling access to the identified content if the claim is valid.
  5. Notify the user (if applicable) that content has been removed due to a DMCA claim.

Please note that we are not in a position to adjudicate copyright ownership disputes. If there is a factual dispute, we may require additional documentation before taking action.

5. Counter-Notice Procedure

If you believe content was removed from XUDOMovie in error or as a result of mistaken identification, you may submit a counter-notice under 17 U.S.C. § 512(g).

Your counter-notice must be sent to dmca@xudomovie.us and must include:

  • Your full name, mailing address, telephone number, and email address.
  • Identification of the material that was removed and its location on the site before removal.
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good-faith belief that the material was removed as a result of mistake or misidentification.
  • A statement that you consent to the jurisdiction of the Federal District Court for the judicial district in which your address is located (or, if outside the US, any judicial district in which XUDOMovie may be found).
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

If we receive a valid counter-notice, we may reinstate the removed content after 10–14 business days unless the original complainant notifies us that they have filed a court action seeking an injunction.

6. Repeat Infringer Policy

In accordance with the DMCA and other applicable law, XUDOMovie maintains a policy to disable or terminate, in appropriate circumstances, access for users who are determined to be repeat infringers. We reserve the right to make this determination at our sole discretion.

7. Abuse of the DMCA Process

We take the abuse of the DMCA takedown process very seriously. Filing a false or misleading DMCA notice is a violation of law and may subject the filer to liability under 17 U.S.C. § 512(f) for damages, including costs and attorneys' fees incurred by the alleged infringer as a result of the false notice.

We reserve the right to report any abuse of the DMCA process to appropriate authorities and to seek all available legal remedies.
